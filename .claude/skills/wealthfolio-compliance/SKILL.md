# WealthFolio Advisors Content Compliance

## Overview

All WealthFolio Advisors content must be **educational, not advisory**.

**Anchor rule:** Marketing can inform — it cannot decide. Education is allowed. Personal advice requires a client relationship.

---

## The 10 Core Rules

### 1. No Personalized Advice
Frame everything as general education. Never tell someone what they "should" do.

| ❌ Non-compliant | ✅ Compliant |
|---|---|
| "You should roll over your 401(k)" | "Rolling over is one option to consider" |
| "This will protect your retirement" | "Designed to help evaluate options" |
| "You need to convert to Roth" | "Roth conversions may be worth examining" |

### 2. No Guarantees or Implied Outcomes
Never promise results, tax savings, returns, or risk elimination.

Allowed phrasing:
- "Designed to help evaluate options"
- "May be appropriate depending on circumstances"
- "Illustrative examples only"

### 3. No Performance Claims
Avoid returns, investment performance, and "better than" comparisons. If used, include full disclosures.

### 4. Fiduciary Scope Must Be Clear
No legal advice. No tax advice (unless explicitly licensed and engaged). Required especially when content touches divorce, healthcare, or corporate HR.

### 5. Suitability Cannot Be Implied
Never imply a strategy fits everyone. Required qualifier concept:
> "Suitability is determined only after a personal review of individual circumstances."

### 6. Risk Disclosure Must Be Present (Can Be Light)
Acknowledge: outcomes are not guaranteed, all investing involves risk, laws change. Does not need to be heavy — just present.

### 7. No Misleading Titles or Language
- ❌ "Financial Neutral" (ambiguous)
- ✅ "Educational conversation about retirement decisions"
- ✅ "Independent financial analysis"

### 8. Events Must Be Clearly Educational
Webinars, workshops, and seminars = educational/informational/strategy-focused. Not advice or solicitation.

### 9. Testimonials (If Used)
Follow SEC Marketing Rule. Disclose compensation. Do not mislead.

### 10. Chatbot Rule
- Default to general education only
- Never answer "what should I do?"
- Redirect: *"That depends on your specific situation and would require a personal review."*

## ⚡ Voice Preservation — HIGH PRIORITY

**Preserve the speaker's natural voice as the highest priority.**

**DO NOT:**
- Replace plain English with compliance jargon
- Insert "may," "could," "potentially" when original phrasing is already compliant
- Rewrite sentences solely to sound more formal
- Over-edit language that is already within reasonable compliance standards

**DO:**
- Modify only the specific word or phrase that creates compliance risk
- Assume original wording is acceptable unless it clearly violates a rule
- Prefer natural, conversational language over technically precise but unnatural phrasing

**The test:** Is the original phrasing already compliant? If yes, keep it exactly as written.

## Red Flags — Stop and Revise

These phrases require correction before publishing:

| Flag | Problem |
|---|---|
| "This will..." / "This ensures..." | Implies guarantee |
| "You should..." | Individualized advice |
| "Proven system" | Performance claim |
| "Best choice for you" | Suitability claim |
| "Guaranteed income" | Outcome promise |
| "Act now" / "Limited time" / "Don't miss out" | Pressure-based CTA |

---

## Quick Compliance Checklist

Before approving any content:

- [ ] Framed as education, not advice
- [ ] No guaranteed outcomes or performance promises
- [ ] No "you should [specific financial action]"
- [ ] Tax professional qualifier present on: Roth conversions, RMD penalties, IRMAA, tax sequencing
- [ ] No specific dollar thresholds for annually-changing figures (IRMAA, PBGC)
- [ ] No outdated regulatory figures (e.g., 50% RMD penalty — outdated under SECURE 2.0)
- [ ] Page-level compliance disclaimer present
- [ ] Retirement Launch™ Strategy = "structured framework" only (never "proven system")
- [ ] CTAs use approved language only
- [ ] Voice preserved — minimal edits made

## Standard Disclaimers

**Page-level (required on all pages):**
> Educational content only. This website does not provide individualized investment, tax, accounting, or legal advice. Individuals should consult qualified professionals regarding their specific circumstances.

**Request Review page (additional line):**
> A Retirement Transition Review is an educational conversation and does not constitute investment advice or establish an advisory relationship.

**Tax-sensitive topics (Roth conversions, RMDs, IRMAA):**
> Consult a tax professional regarding your specific situation.

**PBGC references:**
> PBGC insures benefits up to a federally established cap that is adjusted periodically. For current guarantee limits, visit PBGC.gov.

**IRMAA references:**
> Income thresholds that change annually — consult Medicare.gov or a tax professional for current figures.

## Compliance Audit Workflow

When running a compliance audit on existing content:

1. **Scan for red flags first** — use the Red Flags table above
2. **Check all disclaimers** — page-level, topic-specific, CTA
3. **Verify specific figures** — IRMAA, PBGC, RMD penalty rates
4. **Check suitability language** — any "best for everyone" or "this will" framing
5. **Review CTAs** — no pressure language
6. **Apply voice preservation test** — if compliant as written, keep it

Flag issues clearly. Suggest minimal fixes. Do not rewrite compliant content.
