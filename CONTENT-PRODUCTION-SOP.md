# RetirementRolloverGuide.com Content Production SOP

## Project Objective

RetirementRolloverGuide.com is an educational retirement transition website powered by WealthFolio Advisors, LLC.

The website exists to help individuals approaching retirement evaluate important retirement transition decisions before making major financial choices.

### The website is NOT:

- A generic financial advisor website
- A rollover-focused website
- A product-focused website
- A sales funnel

### The website IS:

- A retirement education center
- A retirement transition resource
- A trusted guide for retirement decision-making
- An authority platform supporting consultation requests

---

## Core Positioning

Every page should reinforce:

**"Retirement involves more than rolling over a 401(k)."**

Common retirement decisions include:

- Retirement income planning
- Pension elections
- Roth conversion considerations
- Distribution planning
- Tax planning considerations
- Social Security timing
- Retirement account decisions
- Retirement transition planning

---

## Global Content Requirements

Every page must include:

### EEAT Elements

- Aly McFarlane introduction where appropriate
- Powered by WealthFolio Advisors, LLC references
- Educational tone
- No exaggerated claims
- No guarantees
- No performance promises

### Writing Style

**Target reading level:** Grade 8–9

**Use:**
- Short paragraphs
- Clear explanations
- Practical language

**Avoid:**
- Industry jargon
- Technical explanations without definitions
- Dense financial language

Every educational page should follow:

1. SEO Title
2. Meta Description
3. H1
4. Direct Answer
5. Key Takeaways

7. FAQ Section
8. Request Review CTA
9. Retirement Checklist CTA
10. Compliance Disclaimer

---

## AEO Requirements

Every page should contain:

### Direct Answer Section

**Word count:** 40–75 words

**Purpose:** Provide an answer suitable for:
- Google AI Overviews
- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Perplexity

**Structure:** Definition + Why It Matters

### Key Takeaways Box

**Format:** 3–6 bullets

**Characteristics:**
- Scannable
- Action-oriented
- Suitable for featured snippets

### FAQ Section

**Minimum:** 6 FAQs

**Structure:**
- Question as H3
- Answer: 50–120 words
- Use natural language search queries

**Example:**
- ✓ "What should I do with my old 401(k) when I retire?"
- ✗ "401(k) rollover options"
