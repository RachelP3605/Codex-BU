## Core Brand Positioning

RetirementRolloverGuide.com is an educational retirement transition website powered by WealthFolio Advisors, LLC.

Primary positioning: Helping individuals approaching retirement evaluate important retirement transition decisions before making major financial choices.

### The Site is NOT:
- A generic financial advisor website
- A rollover-only website
- A lead generation funnel
- Product-focused

### The Site IS:
- A retirement education center
- A retirement decision resource
- A retirement transition planning resource
- An authority platform

### Key Message

**"Retirement involves more than rolling over a 401(k)."**

# CONTENT PRODUCTION SOP

## Global Content Requirements

Every page must include:

### EEAT Elements

- Aly McFarlane introduction where appropriate
- Powered by WealthFolio Advisors, LLC references
- Educational tone
- No exaggerated claims
- No guarantees
- No performance promises

### Writing Style

**Target reading level:** Grade 8–9

**Use:**
- Short paragraphs
- Clear explanations
- Practical language

**Avoid:**
- Industry jargon
- Technical explanations without definitions
- Dense financial language

---

## Universal Page Structure

Every educational page should follow:

1. SEO Title
2. Meta Description
3. H1
4. Direct Answer
5. Key Takeaways
6. Educational Content
7. FAQ Section
8. Request Review CTA
9. Retirement Checklist CTA
10. Compliance Disclaimer

---

## AEO Requirements

Every page should contain:

### Direct Answer Section

**Word count:** 40–75 words

**Purpose:** Provide an answer suitable for:
- Google AI Overviews
- ChatGPT
- Gemini
- Perplexity

**Structure:** Definition + Why It Matters

### Key Takeaways Box

**Format:** 3–6 bullets

**Characteristics:**
- Scannable
- Action-oriented
- Suitable for featured snippets

### FAQ Section

**Minimum:** 6 FAQs

**Structure:**
- Question as H3
- Answer: 50–120 words
- Use natural language search queries

**Example:**
- ✓ "What should I do with my old 401(k) when I retire?"
- ✗ "401(k) rollover options"
