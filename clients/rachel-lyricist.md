# Client: Rachel — Lyricist / Sync Catalog

Related: [[The Script]] [[Memory Loop]]

## Who she is
Rachel writes lyrics and monetizes music through sync licensing. She works with session singers and producers via Musiversal to produce finished tracks. Currently learning Ableton Live 12 Suite but is a non-producer — goal is to do more production herself to reduce costs.

## Business model
- Write lyrics → hire producers/singers via Musiversal → host on DISCO → pitch to Music Supervisors
- Revenue goal: sync placements in TV, film, ads, and other media

## Team
- **Killian Cruise** — producer
- **Peter Krueger (PK)** — producer; handles all vocal comping and tuning
- **Max** — Open Claw AI agent; handles research and execution tasks

## Tech stack
- DISCO (catalog hosting and sync licensing pitches)
- Ableton Live 12 Suite (learning — non-producer, looking for tools to maximize it)
- Musiversal (session singers and producers)
- SUNO (AI song generation — Rachel loads lyrics, gets full AI mockup, then recreates with producers)

## Production workflow
1. Write lyrics
2. Load into SUNO → get full AI mockup
3. Use SUNO version to test direction and share with producers
4. Recreate with Killian or PK using real singers/production

## Ableton AI tools under evaluation
Goal: find tools that control Ableton, load instruments, write MIDI, and arrange — so Rachel can produce without being a producer.

| Tool | Verdict | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| BeatMind | YES — strongest | Creates 8 tracks, loads DS Kick/Clap, Wavetable, Drift, programs patterns, adds EQ/compression/reverb. Clearest promise. |
| VIXSOUND | YES | Generates MIDI, loads instruments from Ableton library, can arrange/mix inside Ableton. |
| GrooveLab | YES-ish | Drives Ableton, writes MIDI, builds tracks with Operator. Sound comes from your own instruments/plugins. |
| ProducersGPT | Probably yes | Claims full DAW control — tracks, clips, MIDI, devices, effects, plugins/samples. Less explicit than BeatMind. |
| Yuma | No — not right fit | MIDI + track/effect control, but sound design "coming soon." Not a fit for current needs. |

**Critical gap:** Open Claw found no meaningful independent reviews for any of these tools — only brand-created content. Do not commit money until real user reviews or a free trial can validate the claims.

## Social Testing Strategy

### Core positioning
Do NOT brand as "AI songs." Brand as: Rachel the songwriter testing song versions and looking for the right voice.

**Public language:** "I'm a songwriter testing different versions of songs before choosing what to produce. This is version 1. Which direction should this song go?"

**If asked about AI:** "It's a production mockup for testing the song. I'm the writer."

**Never say:** "official version," "my new single." No full-song public releases. No presenting AI vocals as a final artist/master.

### Platforms
- YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, Facebook
- TikTok: optional only, do not depend on it

### Paid testing assumptions
- Treat as market research, not content marketing
- Budget: $150–$300 to start
- Organic warm-up first if possible
- Boost only clips with signs of life: $10–$15 per clip initial spend
- Kill weak clips fast. Add $25–$50 only to best performers

### Metrics that matter
- Saves, comments with opinions, shares, completion rate
- "Where can I hear the full song?", "Who sings this?", "I'd cut this"
- Follows from the post, cost per save/comment/share
- **Ignore:** vanity likes as primary metric
