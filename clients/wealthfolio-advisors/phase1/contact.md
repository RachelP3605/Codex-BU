## Frequently Asked Questions

### What does WealthFolio Advisors specialize in?

WealthFolio Advisors, LLC specializes in retirement transition planning — the process of converting retirement savings into sustainable, tax-efficient income. The firm focuses on the distribution phase of financial planning, addressing the decisions that arise in the years just before and after retirement. These include 401(k) rollovers, pension elections, Social Security timing, Roth conversions, income sequencing, and related areas. The firm is based in Long Beach, California, and serves clients throughout Southern California. [Retirement transition mistakes to understand →](/retirement-transition-mistakes/)

### How do I request a Retirement Transition Review?

You can request a Retirement Transition Review using the form on this page or the [Request a Review page](/request-review/), or by contacting WealthFolio Advisors directly using the contact information above. The review is an educational conversation. There is no cost and no obligation to continue or take any action as a result of the conversation.
